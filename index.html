About ICIAET 2025

The International Conference on Innovations and Trends in Advanced Engineering Technologies

Driving engineering breakthroughs through collaboration

ICIAET 2025 convenes pioneering researchers, engineers, startups, policy makers, and industry innovators to explore how intelligent systems, sustainable design, and interdisciplinary collaboration are transforming advanced engineering technologies.

Over two immersive days in November 2025, delegates will share disruptive prototypes, debate the future of automation, experience expert lectures, and fast-track publication opportunities with globally indexed outlets.

Organised by Bioreserach Community (BRC) and Global Institute PN, the conference emphasises practical impact across smart manufacturing, resilient infrastructure, mobility, healthtech, communications, and data-driven engineering.

Join us in shaping the next generation of sustainable, intelligent engineering systems while networking with authors, reviewers, and partners from every continent.

Call for Papers

ICIAET 2025 welcomes original, high-quality submissions across the following tracks.

1. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning in Engineering

  • AI-driven design and optimisation in engineering systems
  • Predictive maintenance & smart manufacturing
  • Edge and cloud AI for industrial automation
  • Explainable AI and ethics in engineering applications

2. Advanced Materials & Nanotechnology

  • Smart materials and intelligent composites
  • Nanomaterials for energy, electronics, and biomedical use
  • Additive manufacturing plus 3D/4D printing
  • Sustainable and recyclable material technologies

3. IoT & Cyber-Physical Systems

  • Industrial IoT and Industry 5.0 applications
  • Digital twins and virtual engineering
  • Cybersecurity in connected systems
  • Sensor networks and edge intelligence

4. Robotics, Automation & Control

  • Autonomous systems and intelligent robotics
  • Human–robot collaboration and cobots
  • Advanced control theory and mechatronics
  • Embedded systems innovations

5. Sustainable & Green Engineering

  • Renewable energy systems and smart grids
  • Energy-efficient and sustainable manufacturing
  • Waste-to-energy conversion and circular economy
  • Environmental impact modelling & mitigation

6. Advanced Communication & Computing

  • 5G/6G networks and next-gen protocols
  • Quantum computing and cryptography
  • High-performance & cloud architectures
  • Embedded and real-time systems

7. Civil, Structural & Smart Infrastructure

  • Smart cities and intelligent transportation
  • Earthquake-resistant & resilient infrastructure
  • Sustainable construction materials and methods
  • BIM and digital construction workflows

8. Biomedical & Healthcare Engineering

  • AI and IoT for healthcare monitoring
  • Biomedical signal & image processing
  • Wearable and implantable medical devices
  • Tissue engineering & bioinformatics

9. Automotive, Aerospace & Transportation

  • Electric and hybrid vehicle technologies
  • Autonomous & connected mobility
  • Advanced propulsion and aerodynamics
  • Smart logistics and transport infrastructure

10. Data Science, Simulation & Computational Engineering

  • Big data analytics for engineering decisions
  • Computational modelling and simulation
  • Digital engineering platforms & cloud design
  • Optimisation algorithms for complex systems

11. Emerging Technologies & Interdisciplinary Innovation

  • Blockchain in engineering and supply chains
  • Augmented/virtual reality for design & education
  • Human-centred and ethical technology design
  • Cross-domain applications of AI and IoT

Call for Papers

Submit your original research to be showcased at ICIAET 2025.

Submission Guidelines

  • Original, unpublished manuscripts not under review elsewhere.
  • Prepared in English using the official conference template.
  • Submitted through the ICIAET online submission portal only.
  • Peer-reviewed by domain experts appointed by the technical committee.
  • Limit of 8 pages per paper (including figures, tables, and references).
